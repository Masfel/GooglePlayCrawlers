Privacy Policy

Thank you for downloading BroadcastMyself app. Here you can read it's the Privacy Policy, all requested permissions description and corresponding comments

No collection of personal information 


We do respect your privacy and doesn't collect any personal information from the application. All records are stored locally on your device and doesn't upload to network, until you don't do it manually by yourself.
