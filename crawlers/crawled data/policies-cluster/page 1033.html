The Types of Personal Information We Use

This section describes the different types of personal information we collect from you and how we collect it. If you would like to know more about specific types of data and how we use that data, please see the section entitled “How we use your personal information” below.

The following is a high level summary of the types of personal information we use:

1. Information you provide to us (either directly or through a third party). 

You give us information about you when you register for the Game (your nominated nickname, and information we import from your connected social media account (Facebook, Google Play or iOS Game Centre) in order to set up your profile, including your name as it appears on your social media profile, your user ID and your profile picture. You also provide us with information when you set up your profile for the Services (including region location and information you voluntarily elect to provide in order to customise gameplay, such as gender), or your nickname when you log-in as a ‘guest’ in order to use the Game Services.

You also provide us with information when you participate in a survey in the Game (in which case we will process the information you provide to us as part of the survey, including Open ID, IP address and, if you choose to provide it, your age, email address, mobile number, name, address and profession). If you choose to log-in using Facebook, Google Play or iOS Game Centre, we import the name of the friends from your social media profile, so that you can connect with them. If you choose to manually add a user as a friend in the Game, we will also store that information so that you can connect with that user. If you choose to provide GPS information, we will use it to allow you to connect with users nearby your location, but your GPS information is not shared with other users.

2. Information about you generated as part of the Game (and its in-Game services)

We automatically collect certain data from you when you use the Game Services, namely your IP address, Open ID and device information (such as device ID, platform and Advertising ID) and information regarding your use of the Game Services, such as Game play statistics (e.g. geographic ranking based on your selected region location). We also collect and store your data:

  • each time you interact with us, for example, when you use in-Game support to communicate with us;
  • if you click on a link that advertises the Game, we store the Advertising ID for that advertisement and marketing analytics data (e.g. device ID, engagement data and search terms) with your profile;
  • if you conduct any transactions within the Game, we collect information to facilitate your transaction and to maintain transaction records (Open ID, cost, product purchased, IP address, device details and, if it is a web-based transaction, your email address);
  • if you contact our support team, we collect information including your name, Open ID, device ID, application version, battery level, WiFi strength, free space on device, iOS version, platform, network type, carrier, country code, and details of the ticket and error. You also have the option of providing your email address and a photo to illustrate the error;
  • when you are using the Game Services, our security feature collects (in addition to the categories of data listed above) devices information including a list of installed hack software, equipment brand and model and available space on the device, and information about your internet connection including the type (WiFi/4G/3G).

3. Information you generate and display in the course of using the Game Services.

You may provide additional information as part of your profile (such as gender). You may also provide or generate information when you communicate with other users in the Game (e.g., chat content and log data). We also generate an Open ID and use this to store your Game data, and we collect and store your Game play statistics (e.g. level, heroes, scores) in order to provide you with feedback and information about your Game play and progress.
