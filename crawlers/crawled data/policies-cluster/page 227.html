Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Vlad Lee. 
 
What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application. 

Ad serving
The Application may use Google's Admob advertising service. Click this link to see Google's privacy policy: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;

with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.
 
What are my opt-out rights?
You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. You can also request to opt-out via email, at privacy@vlmob.com.
 
Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information
We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. We will retain Automatically Collected information for up to 24 months and thereafter may store it in aggregate. If you’d like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Application, please contact us at privacy@vlmob.com and we will respond in a reasonable time.
