Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 December 2019

Thank you for using Rakuten Viki!

Viki, Inc. provides you with an interactive platform that allows you to access television shows, movies and other content, interact with other users and be part of an online community where you may provide subtitles for our content (“Rakuten Viki”, “We”, “Our” and/or “Us”).

Rakuten Viki is part of the Rakuten group, which includes the affiliates and subsidiaries of Our parent company Rakuten, Inc., based in Tokyo, Japan. More information about Rakuten is available at https://global.rakuten.com/corp/about/company (“Rakuten Group”).

This Privacy Policy describes how We collect and process personal information about you, how We use and protect this information, and your rights in relation to this information. Personal Information is information, or a combination of pieces of information that could reasonably allow you or your household to be identified (“Personal Information”).

This Privacy Policy applies when you use Viki.com, Rakuten Viki branded applications and other Rakuten Viki related sites, applications, communications and services (“Services“).

Please read this Privacy Policy, Our Cookies Policy and Our Terms of Use carefully.

  1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

    We will collect Personal Information about you from a variety of sources, including information We collect from you directly, information We automatically collect from your use of the Services, and information We collect about you from other sources, described below:

    1. Information We collect directly from you

      The categories of information that We collect directly from you are:

      1. Identifiers, such as:
        1. Your personal details (for example, full name, gender, age, date of birth and picture). Please note that any personal details included as part of your profile information will be visible to other users of Our Services;
        2. Your contact details (for example, email address); and
        3. Your account login details (for example, your username and password (encrypted));
      2. Financial information, such as your credit card expiry, type and the last 4 digits of the account number (if applicable);
      3. Protected characteristics, such as age and gender. Note that protected characteristics will only be visible to other users of Our Services if voluntarily included by you as part of your profile information;
      4. Commercial information, such as information about your subscription plan(s) and/or service(s) ordered;
      5. Internet or other electronic network activity information, such as information about your online browsing behaviour and preferences;
      6. Visual information, such as your photo; and
      7. Any other information you may choose to provide Us when you post or upload subtitles and/or content to the Services, fill out a form, submit a request to Our community support, respond to a survey, post to forums, submit ratings and reviews and participate in promotions, contests, sweepstakes and/or quizzes.
    2. Information We automatically collect from your use of the Services

      The categories of information that We collect from your use of the Services are:

      1. Identifiers, such as:
        1. Identifiers for devices you have used to access Services (for example, the IP address, a app/device identifier and advertising industry standard unique identifier);
      2. Commercial information, such as certain information about your transactions on the Services (for example, amount charged, date and time) and how you use the Services (for example, pages you view, content you view or favorites, subtitles or segments of content you edit, genres you prefer, searches you make and content consumption history);
      3. Geolocation data, such as city and country
      4. Other device information, such as certain technical information about your device relating to your browser type, language, your operating system and application version, device types, device model and manufacturer and your device operating system type and version;
      5. Cookies and similar technologies, as further described in Our Cookies Policy, We and Our partners automatically collect information from cookies and similar technologies (for example, cookies IDs and settings) to recognize you, track your behaviour on the Services and your device, and to serve you with tailored advertisements. For more information on how We use cookies and how you can manage them, please visit Our Cookies Policy.
    3. Information We collect from other sources:
      1. Registration through third parties

        If you don't feel like creating an account from scratch, We provide you with the option to sign in through an account you already have with another service provider (such as: Facebook, Google and Rakuten ID). The third-party service may send Us information such as:

        1. Identifiers, such as:
          1. Your personal details (for example, name and date of birth); and
          2. Your contact details (for example, email address);
        2. Geographic location;
        3. Visual information, such as profile picture; and
        4. Commercial information, such as interest, list of friends and any other information you have made available on these third-party services.

        This information is controlled by the third-party service or as authorized by you via your privacy settings on the third-party service;

      2. Social media third parties

        Some of Our Services allow you to post information on a public feature of Our Services or through social media sites, plugins or other applications. Don't forget this information is public on Our Services and depending on your privacy settings, may also become public on the internet. We may also further use such public information you share for various reasons such as improving Our Services and providing you with relevant content. You can control what data you share through privacy settings available on some social media sites. Please refer to those third-party privacy policies and terms of use to learn more about their privacy practices, as We don't control these; and

      3. Other sources

        We may receive additional information about you from third party service providers and/or partners, such as demographic data, your activities on and off Our Services through partnerships and Our third party advertisers.
