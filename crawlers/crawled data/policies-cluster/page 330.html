We receive and store certain types of usage related information whenever you interact with Application. For example, HeKu IT GmbH may automatically receive and record information regarding your computer’s IP address, browser information, Facebook Page fan status, and URLs accessed. We use Google Analytics, a analysis service of Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses so-called "cookies", text files which are stored on your device and which enable us to analyze your use of Reading Trainer. The information generated by the cookie on your use of Reading Trainer (including your abbreviated IP address) is transmitted to a Google server in the US and stored there. We will use this information to assess the use of Reading Trainer and compile reports on the activities. It is also possible that Google may transmit this information to third parties if this is prescribed by law or if third parties process this information on behalf of Google. You can deactivate Google Analytics by means of a browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=de
