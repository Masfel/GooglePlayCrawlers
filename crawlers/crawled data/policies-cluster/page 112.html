ZArchiver privacy policy

ZArchiver does not collect, transfer, sell personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or credit card. ZArchiver store archive passwords for some time to access encrypted files. Saved password are stored encrypted, but a hack-proof confidentiality is not guaranteed.

All Requested permissions with explanation

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - to read and write files on external storage (SD card, USB flash);
WAKE_LOCK - to proper work of long-lasting operations;
INSTALL_PACKAGES - to install APK files in Android 8+.
