Privacy policy
Reader for books and documents - ReadEra

1. General provisions

1.1 The ReadEra application does not require the user to provide any personal or confidential information and does not collect it during the use of the application.

1.2 To open books and documents, display them in the application lists, and search them, the system permission to access files is required. The application does not modify, move, or forward files.

1.3 To improve and ensure stable operation, the application can send anonymous application usage statistics. To obtain and analyze anonymous statistics the app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics services provided by Google, Inc. Anonymous statistics can be disabled in the application settings.

2. Providing information to third parties

The application does not transfer the received information to third parties.

3. Contact information

If you have any questions, you can contact us at: support@readera.ru
