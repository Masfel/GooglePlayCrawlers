M2Catalyst Privacy Policy

Last updated Aug 6, 2020

Welcome.

The aim of this privacy policy is to provide an overview of the privacy practices exercised by M2Catalyst, LLC (“M2,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), with respect to our Services (services offered by us including our mobile applications, websites, and related services), unless the terms and conditions or privacy policy accompanying an individual Service indicate otherwise. M2Catalyst is the data controller for the personal data collected through its Services.

We want you to understand our policies and practices when it comes to how we collect, use, and share your information. Your privacy is important to us. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and informing you about how we use it.

By using any of our Services, you hereby agree with the practices set out in this Privacy Policy.


What information does M2 obtain and how is it used?

Information You Provide

Some of our Services require you to provide us with personal information such as your name and email address when you choose to contact us. We require this information in order to respond to your inquiries. IP address may be collected from website logging. Name, email address and external IP address are not collected automatically from any of our Android Apps.

Automatically Collected Information

We gather certain information automatically from our Services including, but not limited to, the model of mobile device you use, your device location, the mobile network (mobile carrier) you use, your mobile network information, language, and Wi-Fi network information.

We use the collected information for the administration and operation of our Services and to generally improve the overall quality of your experience. We also assign a unique M2 identifier to your device, which is not associated with any external databases or third-party identifiers.

We also use cookies on our websites to permit us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information.

Data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve our Services. We will share data collected through our Services with third parties only in the ways that are described in this Privacy Policy.


We may disclose Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • with our trusted web hosting, cloud services, and data storage services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement;
  • if M2Catalyst, LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of any of our Services; in such a case, you will be notified via a notification sent to your mobile device, through email, and/or a prominent notice on any related Service of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information;
  • through the sale or license of this information to third party customers in the ordinary course of our business.

We work with analytics companies such as Google and Flurry to help us understand how our Services are being used, such as the frequency and duration of usage.

Legal Basis for Processing

If you provide consent, we will collect and process personal data about you, as part of a contractual agreement with you, and as necessary to provide our Services to you, operate our business, meet our contractual and legal obligations, protect the security of our Services, and fulfill our other legitimate interests.

Global Data Transfers

Our headquarters is in the United States and information we collect from you will be processed in the United States.

Individuals in the EEA: The United States has not received a finding of “adequacy” from the European Union under Article 45 of the GDPR. We rely on derogations for specific situations set forth in Article 49 of the GDPR in order to transfer your personal information across international borders. In particular, we collect and transfer personal information outside of the EU only: (i) with your consent; (ii) to perform a contract with you; or (iii) to fulfill other compelling legitimate interests in a manner that does not outweigh your rights and freedoms.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can suspend the collection of geolocation information by changing your device, application, or browser settings.

Our websites do not respond to “Do Not Track” signals.

You can permanently stop all collection of information from our Services simply by uninstalling (if such Service is a mobile application installed on your device), or discontinuing use (if such Service is a website or related service). For uninstalling, you may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

Individuals in the EEA: To the extent required by law, we provide you with (i) reasonable access to the personal information collected through our Services, and (ii) the ability to correct, export, suspend processing, or delete personal information. These functions will either be available within your Service(s), or you can contact us at info@m2catalyst.com to submit a request.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as needed : (i) to conduct business with you; (ii) as needed for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or at the time of collection; or (iii) as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce any agreements.
