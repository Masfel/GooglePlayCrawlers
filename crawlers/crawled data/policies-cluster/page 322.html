Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following statement will help you understand how our app deals with the personal identifiable information ('PII') you may occasionally provide to us via Internet (the Google Play Platform).

Generally, we do not collect any PII from you when you download our Android applications. To be specific, we do not require the consumers to get registered before downloading the application

Cookies Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

This Service does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use “cookies” to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this Service.

Information Collection and Use We collect non PII like 1DM+ app package name, app version, cpu arch, country and a unique device identifier to save/download important settings (A notification will be shown with title as Syncing or Syncing settings).

The only situation we may get access to your PII is when you personally decide to email us your feedback or to provide us with a bug report. The PII we may get from you in that situation are strictly limited to your name, email address, device information and your feedback response only.

The app does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you.

How we use your information In above situation, we guarantee that your PII will only be used for contacting you and improving our services. We will never use such information (e.g. your name and email address) for any other purposes, such as to further market our products, or to disclose your personal information to a third party for commercial gains.

App permissions used in 1DM+

  • android.permission.INTERNET
    This permission is required to download files and browse the internet
  • android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
    This permission is required for write access to SD card
  • android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
    This permission is required for read access to SD card
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
    This permission is required to get state of the network
  • android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
    This permission is required to get state of wifi
  • android.permission.CHANGE_WIFI_STATE
    This permission is required to switch on and switch off wifi
  • android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE
    This permission is required to pause/resume videos/ads on incoming or outgoing calls
  • android.permission.VIBRATE
    This permission is required to vibrate the device when download starts, finishes or errors outline
  • android.permission.WAKE_LOCK
    This permission is required to take system wake lock to keep the system active while download is in progress
  • android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
    This permission is required to start the Smart download service (if Smart download is enabled)
  • android.permission.SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW
    This permission is required to show overlay notification if(if overlay notification is enabled)
  • com.android.launcher.permission.INSTALL_SHORTCUT
    This permission is required to create shortcut on home screen
  • com.android.launcher.permission.REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES
    This permission is required to install downloaded apks
  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE
    This permission is required to run service in foreground
  • android.permission.PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS
    This permission is required to get internet data usage for using the data usage limit option
