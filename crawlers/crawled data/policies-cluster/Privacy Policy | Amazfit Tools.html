Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

None. We do not have any internet cloud connection, making it literally impossible to collect anything.

  • We do not collect any privacy information from our users.
  • We do not collect any usage statistics.
  • We do not show any ads.
  • We’re not in the business of selling personal information.

All we know about our customers is what is available in the Google Wallet Payment Details and these information are used only for accounting and tax purposes.
