    Privacy Policy

    ​

    DCGamePub Limited (hereinafter referred to as “DCGamePub”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users and handling any personal data obtained from users with care and respect. An overall principle is that we do not collect more information than is reasonably necessary to provide you with the best user experience.
    This Privacy Policy (hereafter referred to as the "Privacy Policy" or “Policy”)) is applicable where the DCGamePub acts as a data controller with respect to the data we collect from you. This Privacy Policy will explain how we use the personal data we collect from you when you use our mobile game applications (also referred to as the “games” or “game”) or the official web-site of DCGamePub with the domain name https://www.deuscraft.com/ (hereinafter referred to as the “web-site”). Providing access to the games and the web-site is collectively referred to as the “Services”.

     

    1. What is the legal basis for processing your personal data?

    ​

    The legal basis for processing your personal data is your freely given consent.


    When you download and first access the game of DCGamePub on your mobile device, you will see the banner that will offer you to follow the URL links and read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service and will include the button “Confirm”. Before you start using the game of DCGamePub, you shall read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service and press the button “Confirm”. 


    By pressing the button “Confirm”, you provide DCGamePub with express and unambiguous consent to processing of your personal data in accordance with this Policy and the provisions of General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR). 


    Besides, before starting to use the game of DCGamePub you shall put a tick in the box or press the button “Confirm” (depending on the settings of the game) in the section “I confirm that I am at least 18 (eighteen) years old (hereinafter referred to as the “section”). If you are less than 18 (eighteen) years old, you are not allowed to proceed with using the game.


    You cannot start using the game of DCGamePub before providing us with the consent to processing of your personal data. If you do not agree to the processing of your personal data in accordance with our Policy and do not press the button “Confirm” or put a tick in the box, you will not be provided with the opportunity to use the game. In this case we will not process your personal data.


    Processing of your personal data is necessary for providing you with the Services. 


    When you open the web-site of DCGamePub, you have an opportunity to use emails provided for on the web-site for the purpose of contacting us. When your email us, you provide as with express and unambiguous consent to processing of your personal data necessary for providing the answer to your letter.

    3. What personal data do we process and how?

     

    DCGamePub collects information when you start using our Services. This information relates to how you use our Services including the type of device you use, and may include information that you submit when creating an account with us or through your social network accounts.


    Particularly, when you download and start using our game, the SDK integrated into the game receive access to Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) (if the mobile application is distributed via App Store and available on iOS operating system) or Google Advertising ID (if the mobile application is distributed via Google Play and available on Android operating system) of your mobile device (hereinafter referred to as the “Device ID”). Such Device ID tracks all the behavioral information about you that relates to your use of the game. Particularly, by use of Device ID we can automatically receive the information on your progress inside the game, on your interactions with other players of the game, on the virtual items you purchase via the game (either for free or for real or virtual currency), on the rewards you receive via the game, on your scores or achievements via the game, and other gameplay data. For the avoidance of doubts, we do not collect any information on other mobile applications installed on your mobile device. 


    Besides, the SDK integrated into the game allow DCGamePub to automatically collect the following data:

    • IP address of the device;

    • device name and operating system;

    • language settings;

    • precise location data (your actual GPS position), if you provide us with the consent to processing of such personal data

    ​

    Whether or not you create an account inside the game, we can receive the following information from you: 

    • language preferences; 

    • music and sound settings;

    • player’s nickname;

    • the information on the amount of money you spend on in-app purchases

    ​

    In order to save the progress of the game (gameplay data) on your mobile device, you have an option to create an account via the game. You can create the account by registering inside the game or by linking the game to your social network account (such as Google Play account, Facebook page etc.). In this case we can process the following personal data that you provide to us: 

    • ID of your social network account (Google ID, Facebook ID etc.); the social network that holds your social network account can also share your GPS position or general location data (the country of your residence) with us if your account settings allow to do so;

    • your real name or nickname;

    • the photo of your social network profile;

    • your age and gender;

    • your contact information (such as email, phone number etc.)

    ​

    Besides, you can write a message to DCGamePub through our games. In this case we can process the information you provide to us and the information on chat logs and player support tickets. Additionally, we can process the information you provide to us which helps to identify your lost account of the game. 


    If you exchange messages with other players via the game, DCGamePub may also store your conversation history with other players, including chat logs. 

    ​

    If you send a complaint to us stating that you in-app purchase was not successful (for example, you did not receive the virtual item you had purchased, or the system debited the excessive amount of money from your account) or ask us to delete or restore you in-game account, we can process the following personal data: 

    • identification information on your in-game account (including Facebook or Google ID); 

    • payment details (meaning the information about the transaction itself); we do not process the information on your bank account or credit/debit card


    We can also process other types of information that you willfully provide to us in order to verify your in-game account. 


    If you do not provide us with the identification information on your in-game account, payment details, or other types of information necessary to identify your account, DCGamePub reserves the right not to resolve your issue (refuse to delete or restore your account or reply to your complaint on in-app purchase), subject that it is not technically possible to resolve your issue without processing your personal data. 

    ​

    If you email us via the web-site, we can store the history of conversation with you and use your message and the information from the message to provide you with the answer to the message. 


    We do not knowingly process any cookies via our web-site. However, our web-site was designed by use of the Wix service, and Wix may process some cookie files. We do not control such process because Wix provides templates for creating web-sites and controls the use of such templates. To learn more about Wix and their processing of cookies, please follow the official web-site of Wix with the domain name wix.com.

    7.What are your data protection rights? 

     

    DCGamePub would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every personal data subject is entitled to the following:


    The right to access – You have the right to request us for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service. Besides, you have the right to request DCGamePub to provide you with the information on whether your personal data is being processed, and on the aspects of processing your personal data (namely, the information about the purposes of personal data processing, the categories of personal data processed, the categories of recipients of your personal data, the period during which your personal data is processed, the sources from which your personal data is collected, and your rights that you granted in accordance with GDPR). 


    The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete the information you believe is incomplete.


    The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.


    The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.


    The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to ours processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.


    The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.


    You may send the request aimed at exercising your rights provided herein to our email or to our business address: Diagorou 4, KERMIA BUILDING, 2nd floor, Flat/Office 204, 1097, Nicosia, Cyprus. 


    If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of your rights, please state clearly in the request what the information you would like to know, rectify, or erase. This request needs to be signed and dated and contain a copy of the front side of your identity document.


    You can also withdraw your consent to processing of your personal data. Please note that if you do so and do not have the access to the data that we need to maintain and administer the Services, you may not be able to use the Services and/or get registered at it.


    Besides, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority of the state of your habitual residence, or of the state where your work place is located, or of the state where the place of alleged infringement of GDPR is located, if you consider that processing of your personal data by DCGamePub or its processors infringes GDPR. Exercising of such right does not preclude you from using other administrative or judicial remedies. 


    If you are not satisfied with the decision of the supervisory authority, you have the right to an effective judicial remedy against the decision of such supervisory authority. The exercising of such right depends on the procedural law of the state in which the supervisory authority is established. 


    You also have the right to an effective judicial remedy against DCGamePub or its processors if you consider that processing of your personal data by DCGamePub or its processors infringes GDPR. In this case the lawsuit against DCGamePub or its processors shall be brought before the courts of the states where DCGamePub or its processors are established, or, alternatively, before the court of the state of your habitual residence.
