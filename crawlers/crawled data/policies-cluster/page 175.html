In addition, agreements or notices associated with a particular Rave product, service, software or website (“Rave Offering”) may provide additional information about Rave’s processing of your personal information (“Notice”). By using our Services, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Please read and review this Privacy Policy carefully before using or accessing our Services and check our Service and Privacy Policy from time to time to inform yourself of any changes to this Privacy Policy or any other policies or agreements. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please refer to the end of this Privacy Policy for information on how to contact us.

1. Information we collect about you

a) In General: Personal information is information about an identifiable individual (as defined by applicable privacy or data protection laws). We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, Rave unique identifier, and other information that does not identify you. By using the Services, you authorize us to collect, parse and retain information related to the provision of the Service.

b) Information you provide: In order to register as a user of Rave, you will be asked to sign in using your Facebook login. If you choose to login to Rave via your Facebook login, you authorize us to access the following Facebook account information: public Facebook profile and information (consistent with your privacy settings in Facebook), your email address, friend list, Facebook friends you might disclose in common with other Rave users, and public profile photos of your Facebook friends who also have Rave. In addition, we may collect and store any personal information you provide while using our Services, or in some other manner. This may include identifying information, such as your name, address, email address and telephone number, and if you transact business with us, financial information, and content and information you post, upload, publish, link to, transmit, record, display or otherwise make available on the Services, or transmit to other users, including text messages, chat, videos (including streaming videos), photographs, or profile text, whether publicly or privately transmitted or posted. If you contact us with a customer service or other inquiry, you provide us with the content of that communication.

c) Use of technologies to collect information: We may use various technologies to collect information from your device and about your activities on our Service

d) Information collected automatically: We automatically collect information from your browser or mobile device when you visit, access, or use our Services. This information could include your IP address, device ID and type, browser type and language, operating system used by your device, access times, and the referring website address.

e) Cookies and Use of Cookie Data: When you visit, access, or use our Services, we may assign your device one or more cookies in order to facilitate or personalize your access and utilization of our Services. Through the use of a cookie, we may automatically collect information about your activity on our Services, such as the pages you visit, the time and date of your visits, and the links you click. If we advertise, we (or third parties) may use certain data collected on our Services to show you Rave advertisements on other sites or applications. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser settings to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. You can typically remove or block browser cookies using the settings in your browser, but that may impact your ability to use certain Rave Offerings as they may not work well or at all with cookies disabled. Choosing to continue without disabling cookies indicates your consent to Rave's use of cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

f) Other Technologies: We may use standard internet technology, including but not limited to web beacons and other similar technologies to track your use of our Services and to deliver or communicate with cookies. These technologies may also be used in advertisements or email messages in order to determine whether messages have been opened or acted upon. This information that is collected helps us to optimize our overall content, programming, and advertising activities.

g) Information collected by third-parties for advertising purposes: We may allow service providers, advertising companies, ad networks and other third parties to display advertisements on our Service. These third parties may use tracking technologies such as cookies or web beacons, to collect information about users who access, view, or interact with their advertisements. Rave may provide non-anonymized personal information to third parties.
