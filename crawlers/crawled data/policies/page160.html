1. GENERAL INFORMATION

1.1. Types of Users: Sparkle is targeted to the following types of users:

1.1.1. "Visitors": Anyone who browses Sparkle without registering. Visitors are only allowed to view Sparkle posts.

1.1.2. "Users": Anyone who logs onto Sparkle with an account created via Sparkle registration e/or through a link with a Hotmart account.

1.2. Access to Posts: Visitors and Users can access videos, audios, images and texts ("Posts") of communities created by Users ("Community"). However, only Users can interact with these Posts and with other Users through comments.

1.3. Sharing with third parties: Through this Policy, we want to be transparent about what personal data is processed in Sparkle. We remind you that some of your personal data is shared with third parties, for the effective operation of Sparkle.

2. DATA FROM MINORS UNDER THE AGE OF 13

2.1. Processing the personal data of minors: We do not collect or knowingly request personal data from minors under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor under the age of 13, we will cancel the account created on the Platform and delete the personal data, keeping only personal data for the purpose of preventing another attempt to register. Sparkle users between the ages of 13 and 18 will have their personal data processed according to this Policy.

3. PERSONAL DATA PROCESSED

3.1. Providing your Information: In order to take advantage of Sparkle, you can register and, to do so, you need to provide certain types of personal data. Among other activities, we collect personal data to allow Sparkle’s operation:

3.1.1. Registration Information: This is the information requested from the User when registering on Sparkle and that allows User identification, such as: (a) Full name; (b) Date of Birth; (c) Email; (d) Password; (e) Preferred language.

3.1.2. Registration Data for Events, Newsletter and/or Hotmart Relationships: (a) Name; (b) Email; (c) Other information that Hotmart may request for Events, Newsletter and/or Relationship.

3.1.3. Profile Information: This is the information that Users provide to Sparkle, through Sparkle, after the creation of their registration, in order to complement their profile, such as: (a) Bio; (b) User name; (c) Profile photo.

3.1.4. Activity Information: We collect information related to your Sparkle use, such as: (a) Frequency of Sparkle Use; (b) Information about the User's browser, in case of Web access; (d) Information about the User’s mobile device, in case of use of App access; (e) Product Use Data; (f) User Connection Data.

3.1.5. Post Data: Users’ Posts, as well as the User’s image and voice, among other types of information related to Users.

3.1.6. Support Data: (a) Name; (b) Email; (c) Subject; (d) Current country; (e) Reply language. At your discretion, you may submit other types of supporting documents, information and/or data ("Supplementary Documents"). Sparkle may request Supplementary Documents that will assist in the service.

3.1.7. Hotmart Account Data: The personal data linked to your Hotmart account. For further information, read the Hotmart Privacy Policy.

DEFINITIONS

The terms stated in the item DEFINITIONS in the Terms of Use are used in this Privacy Policy with the same meanings.

Whenever the terms below are used in capital letters in this Privacy Policy, they have the same meaning as expressed in the current legislation.

REGISTRATION DATA: affiliation, address (including electronic address), PERSONAL qualification, understood as name, surname, marital status, and profession from the User.

PERSONAL DATA: information relating to the natural person. Considered all data that can be directly or indirectly used to identify a person, for example: name, photos, email, bank details, medical information, geolocation, phone number, posts on social media, computer IP address, etc.

PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA: every operation made with PERSONA DATA, such as the ones relating to the collection, production, reception, classification, use, access, reproduction, transmission, distribution, processing, storage, deletion, assessment, or control of information, modification, communication, transference, diffusion, or extraction.
